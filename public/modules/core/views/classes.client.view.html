Kid's Classes.

The kids class is the future at Outliers Jiu-Jitsu. The class is taught by a qualified Ribeiro Jiu-Jitsu instructor, in a child friendly manner. In addition to teaching the children the fundamental technique, the kids class incorporates strength and conditioning to help keep your child strong and healthy. The kids class is not just a bunch of boring instruction and drills, functional games are incorporated into the curriculum for fun and inspiration. At Outliers Jiu-Jitsu, our goal is not only to teach the Jiu-Jitsu technique in a safe and fun manner, but the kids class tries to lift-up the emotional and mental character of the children so they are successful not only on the Jiu-Jitsu mat but in all aspects of life.
